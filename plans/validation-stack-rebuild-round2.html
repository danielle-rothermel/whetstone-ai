What must be true when this branch is accepted?

contract

A cell resumes only its exact optimizer, environment, and attempt. Its serialized optimization control is bound before any step or terminal result can be reused. Every paid official arm and optimization step has durable evidence, and each new paid boundary checks a fresh credits snapshot before proceeding.

Acceptance requires one coherent path through cell orchestration, optimization control, and the durable harness.

Which identities prevent cross-run reuse?

identity

Cell identity

The run id equals optimizer:environment:attempt. Ledger lookup names the same three fields, so a later attempt cannot satisfy an earlier resume.

Run-control identity

Candidate records, budget, algorithm parameters, evaluation configs, tools, and proposer or reflection config compose one persisted control. Every step request carries that control’s content hash.

Adapter identity

COPRO, MIPROv2, and GEPA expose their configured proposer boundary. The controller validates it against the serialized control before binding or replaying a run.

Where does the budget gate stop additional spend?

budget

The cell records a fresh checkpoint before each official arm and before every unresolved non-pure optimization step. Cumulative spend is measured from the cell’s initial snapshot. Crossing the configured stop-loss raises before the next transport, proposer, evaluation, tool, or opaque-agent boundary.

Durable replays and pure identity steps do not create paid-step checkpoints.
